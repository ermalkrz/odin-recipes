Chicken Alfredo

Description

This easy slow cooker chicken Alfredo with Swiss cheese and Parmesan is super creamy and saucy. It's so good served over egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese/Sauce
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil noodles.
  2. Prepare sauce.
  3. Cook Chicken
  4. Combine and serve.
